Justin's Smilly But Yummy Belachan

INGREDIENT'S

  • 1k of Banana
  • 1k of Tomato
  • 1 Belachan Cube
  • Salt and Magic Sarap
  • Greeen chilli
  • Garlic

Cooking Tools

  • Spatula
  • Deep Frying Pot
  • Strainer
  • Food Processor
  • Meduim Size Pan

For the Sauace

  1. Slice the Tomato in Half and Remove the Seeds
  2. Peel and Slice the Garlic in Half
  3. Saute the Tomato and Garlic
  4. Then Process it in the Food Processor
  5. Put it Back in the Pan Put the Belachan Cube Pinch of Salt and Magic Sarap
  6. If the Taste is Good Let it Cool

For the Banana

  1. Peel and Slice into Thin Peices
  2. Place it Outside to Dry For About 2 Hours
  3. After That Fry the Banana in a Low Heat
  4. Fry untill Golden Brown
  5. Put it in a Strainer to Remove the Acces Oil
